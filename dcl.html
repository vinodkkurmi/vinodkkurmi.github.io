Visual Data Computing Group (VisDom)

About the Group The research areas associated with this group are computer vision (CV), deep learning (DL), and machine learning (ML). Some currently going projects are fairness in CV, domain genelization, audio domain adaptation, source-free domain adapation, computer vision in medical data, social awareness detection using DL and CV, etc.

We are looking for motivated researchers with a keen interest in computer vision, machine learning, and deep learning. Interested inside or outside of IISER Bhopal can mail their CV to me highlighting their areas of interest and/or experience. We have openings for junior research fellow/projects associates and PhDs in deep learning-based computer vision projects.

Useful links: IISER Bhopal Internship Program, PhD Program, Dissertation form Non-IISERB
